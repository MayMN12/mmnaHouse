Selling service

We provide assistance for selling your home.
We understand you're not an property expert. We provide real estate solutions to help you maximize your chances of success. Our team will take professional photos & videos, handle negotiations and close the deal at your target price. After the deal is complete, our friendly team of lawyers will handle the paperwork.


Professional Photography

Professional photos and videos are what'll get a prospective buyer to visit your property. We offer this service for free to all Sellers who sign up with us.


Marketing

Once our photographers are finished, we need to reach a prospective buyer. We will marketing your property through - Email marketing, PPC & Content marketing.


Negotiations

After the viewings are complete, we'll follow up with all prospects and handle negotiations on your behalf. On average it takes between 5 to 10 working days to close a deal.
