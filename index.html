Our story
FRL started its operations in Myanmar in 2019, under the FRL brand. Since then, we have expanded our services to offer a full spectrum of solutions for our clients. Our core focus has and always will be, helping our clients enhance their success in Myanmar.

When moving to a new country, the real estate broker will be one of your first perceptions of a country. From this belief, we believe it is our duty to provide a professional, reliable, and efficient service to bring coherence to Myanmar's Real Estate Market.

Our core services are Sales & Rental, but through the years we have expanded our services to provide cleaning, pest control, renovation, relocation, and property management services. Through these solutions, FRL is a one-stop solution you can rely on.

We provide free consultations for all our services, if you would like to book an appointment feel free to contact us.
